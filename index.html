multilogues
A machine’s monologue, directed towards a human.
A machine and a gerbil having a dialogue about eachothers’ dreams.
A machine, a gerbil and a psychotherapist having a nonlinear, linear, trialogue.
A cybernetic artifact that, in a series of multilogues, explores the concepts of language, communication, and interaction between human and machine, as well as their places in the new paradigm shift of the real and virtual world (if such separation can be made). These constrains are declared, first, by the unheard views of a machine, followed by the technological dreams of a gerbil and concluded by the (failed) attempt of a psychotherapist to serve as mediator between the two.
